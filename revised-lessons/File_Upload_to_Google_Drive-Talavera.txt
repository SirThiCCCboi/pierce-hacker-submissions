## Overview
What is Google Drive? Google Drive is a cloud based storage that stores your files when needed.
What is uploading files onto Google Drive? Uploading files onto Google Drive is when you store your certain file onto the google drive storage.
Why would you want to upload to Google Drive? Youâ€™d want to upload to Google Drive because it is a simple and easy way to save your files. It is also very accessible by any device as long as you have access to your Google/G-Mail account.
